Project Proposal

About

For this project, I want to create a webpage for a club that I am part of at Babson.

I am the VP of Finance for the Babson Analytics Association. We are a new club that started last fall semester. I want to create this webpage so that we can recruit more members for our club and have a site that people can see our events and schedule.

Target Audience

My target audience for this page will be Babson students who are interested in analytics or want to learn more about analytics outside of the core curriculum classes.

Content

For content, I would like to post information about data analytics and the possible future careers inside the field. I would also post information about upcoming events, and recordings of past guest speakers.
